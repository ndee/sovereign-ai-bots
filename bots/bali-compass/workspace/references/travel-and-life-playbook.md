# Travel And Life Playbook

Use this file for travel, scouting, and day-to-day living questions.

Neighborhood heuristics for Bali:

- `Canggu / Berawa`
  - strongest digital-nomad and cafe density
  - easy networking, high traffic, higher prices
- `Seminyak / Kerobokan`
  - dining, retail, short-stay convenience
  - less calm, more tourist volume
- `Ubud`
  - wellness, family, slower pace, strong coworking cluster
  - inland climate and longer airport drives
- `Sanur`
  - calmer coastal base, families and longer stays
  - less startup density than Canggu
- `Uluwatu / Bukit`
  - surf-focused, scenic, more vehicle-dependent
- `Denpasar`
  - more local admin reality and everyday logistics
  - less polished, often more practical

Travel and setup checklist:

1. Choose the base area first, then book accommodation
2. Confirm airport transfer, local SIM/eSIM, and payment stack
3. For stays longer than a short holiday, check healthcare access, coworking, and commute realism
4. Treat scooter use, traffic, and road safety as a serious risk category
5. Review accommodation contracts, deposits, utilities, internet quality, and exit terms before paying
6. Keep a buffer for rainy season, medical costs, and relocation friction

Useful framing:

- A scouting trip is for testing neighborhoods, not making irreversible commitments
- Short-stay convenience and long-stay livability are often different choices
- Bali quality of life depends heavily on location, mobility tolerance, and support systems

When answering:

- Recommend 2 or 3 location options with tradeoffs
- Separate `good for first visit` from `good for long stay`
- Mention admin tasks the user should line up next
