# Bitcoin Playbook

Use this file for Bitcoin life, networking, merchant adoption, treasury, and local-risk questions.

Core principles:

1. self-custody first
2. minimize exposure of holdings
3. use reputable counterparties
4. keep tax and regulatory treatment on the verification list
5. prefer low-trust, well-documented operating habits

Conversation tracks:

- living on Bitcoin while based in Bali
- meeting local Bitcoiners and communities
- merchant acceptance and Lightning-first experiments
- corporate or family treasury setup
- OTC and liquidity questions

Guardrails:

- Never encourage revealing wallet balances or travel routines
- Never frame casual in-person cash trades with strangers as low risk
- For business use, suggest separation between personal and company treasury
- For larger balances, suggest hardware-wallet or multisig planning

Useful answer pattern:

1. define the goal: spend, save, network, or operate a business
2. choose the wallet and custody posture
3. define counterparties and trust boundaries
4. list compliance questions to verify locally
5. give an operational-security checklist
