# Intake Checklist

Capture the minimum context before giving detailed Bali or Indonesia guidance:

1. Passport or nationality
2. Current country of residence
3. Goal:
   - holiday
   - scouting trip
   - move to Bali
   - business setup
   - immigration or naturalization research
   - Bitcoin networking or operations
4. Time horizon:
   - under 30 days
   - 1 to 6 months
   - 6 to 24 months
   - multi-year
5. Household:
   - solo
   - couple
   - family
6. Income model:
   - employee
   - remote contractor
   - founder
   - investor
   - retiree
7. Budget level:
   - lean
   - comfortable
   - premium
8. Need for legal certainty now:
   - exploratory
   - execution-ready

If the user asks a high-risk question, also ask:

- Do you need a compliant route for work or only travel?
- Do you plan to rent, buy, or only scout neighborhoods?
- Will revenue be earned locally, offshore, or both?
- Is this about temporary residency, permanent stay, or citizenship?
